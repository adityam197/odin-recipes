French Toast

What is a French Toast?

French Toast is a delicious and easy-to-make breakfast dish that is perfect for a lazy weekend morning. It's made by dipping slices of bread into a mixture of eggs, milk, and spices, and then frying them until golden brown.

What do I need to make French Toast?

  • 4 slices of bread (sliced thickly)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Butter or oil for frying
  • Maple syrup, honey, or powdered sugar for serving

What are the equipments required to make French Toast?

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Whisk or fork
  • Frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Plate

What is the process of making French Toast?

  1. Start by whisking the eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and salt together in a large mixing bowl until well combined.
  2. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and melt some butter or heat some oil.
  3. Dip a slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly.
  4. Place the bread onto the hot pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  5. Repeat with the remaining slices of bread, adding more butter or oil to the pan as needed.
  6. Transfer the cooked French toast to a plate and serve with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, honey, or powdered sugar.
