What is a Omelette?

Omelette is a classic breakfast dish that is quick and easy to make. It's made by whisking eggs with milk, and then cooking them in a frying pan with your choice of fillings. This recipe is versatile and can be customized with your favorite ingredients, making it a perfect breakfast option any day of the week.

What do I need to make Omelette?

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of milk or cream
  • A pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup of diced vegetables (such as onions, peppers, or mushrooms)
  • Optional: fresh herbs or spices, cooked meat, or other fillings

What are the equipments required to make Omelette?

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Whisk or fork
  • Non-stick frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Plate

What is the process of making Omelette?

  1. Start by whisking the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper together in a large mixing bowl until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it cook for about 1 minute until the bottom is set.
  4. Add the shredded cheese and diced vegetables (or other fillings) to one side of the omelette.
  5. Use a spatula to fold the other side of the omelette over the fillings, creating a half-moon shape.
  6. Cook the omelette for an additional 1-2 minutes until the cheese is melted and the egg is cooked through.
  7. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
