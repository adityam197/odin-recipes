Pasta

What is a Pasta?

Homemade pasta is a delicious and comforting dish that is made from scratch with just a few simple ingredients. The dough is made by mixing flour, eggs, and salt together and then kneading it until it becomes smooth and elastic.

After the dough has rested, it's rolled out and cut into the desired shape using a pasta machine or a knife. The pasta is then cooked in boiling salted water until it's tender and cooked to your liking. Homemade pasta is versatile and can be served with a variety of sauces and toppings, making it a perfect dish for any occasion.

What do I need to make Pasta?

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoon of salt

What are the equipments required to make Pasta?

  • Rolling pin
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Clean surface for kneading
  • Plastic wrap or clean kitchen towel
  • Knife or pasta cutter

What is the process of making Pasta?

  1. Start by mixing the flour and salt together in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Create a well in the center of the flour mixture and add the eggs.
  3. Beat the eggs with a fork and slowly start incorporating the flour until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 10 minutes or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap or a clean kitchen towel and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  6. After the dough has rested, cut it into four equal parts and flatten each piece with a rolling pin.
  7. Pass the flattened dough through a pasta machine, starting with the widest setting and gradually making it thinner until the desired thickness is achieved.
  8. Cut the pasta sheets into the desired shape using a knife or pasta cutter.
  9. Cook the pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water for 2-4 minutes or until it's cooked to your liking.
  10. Drain the pasta and toss it with your favorite sauce or toppings.
